Projects

AVAZ inversion of local object anisotropy parameters from 3D seismic data

XII/2019 – VIII/2021

Role: team leader. Team size: 7. Customer: NTC NIS-Naftagas, Serbia.

Technology and software for AVAZ inversion of target object anisotropy parameters from 3D seismic data was developed. Software was embedded as a plugin in Petrel (commonly used software for interpretation). The plugin also allows to process 3D seismic data. It was important to integrate data processing and interpretation stages for undistorted local analysis.

Acoustic properties and internal structure of hydrate-bearing coal samples

VII/2019 – VI/2021

Role: head of the project. Team size: 5. Supported by RSF, grant No. 19-77-00068.

In-situ 3D X-ray tomography, in addition to acoustic measurements, was used for analysis of hydrate-bearing coal samples. The results show more complicated hydrate formation and dissociation processes in comparison with similar experiments on sand samples. A competitive sorption of methane and water in the coal pore space was revealed.

Fractured media modelling using synthetic samples printed on a 3D printer

II/2019 – XII/2021

Role: head of the project. Team size: 4. Supported by RFBR, grant No. 19-05-00730.

FDM and SLA methods of 3D printing were tested for the tast of fractured media modelling. But irregularity of internal structure filling and sintering of the thread in the case of FDM, and irregularity of photopolymer solidification depending on the thickness of internal blocks in the case of SLA printing leads to acoustic heterogeneity of the formed samples.

Laboratory experiments on the formation of gas hydrates in coal samples

II/2017 – XI/2018

Role: head of the project. Team size: 4. Supported by RFBR, grant No. 17-35-80023.

We did a series of experiments on formation of methane hydrate in crashed bituminous coal samples. As a result, the methodology of gas hydrate formation from different types of water in coal samples was developed. Also, a dependence of acoustic properties from temperature and stress was revealed.
